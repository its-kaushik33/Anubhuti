The Founder of Our Organisation

ACHARYA DEEPCHAND KAUSHIK JI

Acharya Deepchand Kaushik is a renowned yoga teacher and founder of Anubhuti Yog Sanstha. He is famous for teaching Yoga through various ways including mass yoga camps, television shows, radio lectures in schools and colleges. He strives to cover all age groups from school-going to elderly. He has been teaching yoga for the last 32 years in India and abroad. Born in Haryana, Acharya ji was keen on learning yoga when he was at school.
